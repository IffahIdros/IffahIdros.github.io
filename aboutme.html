About Me

Learn more about who I am, what I do, and what I am interested in.


Hello! I am currently a second-semester student at UiTM Rembau branch, Negeri Sembilan,
where I am pursuing a degree in Library Management.
My academic journey is equipping me with strong organizational, research, and information management skills
preparing me for various roles in libraries, archives, and information centers.


Beyond my studies, I have a diverse set of interests:


Barista

I love the vibrant, social environment of cafes.
I would love to grab the chance to work as a barista
as it will allows me to hone my customer service skills,
attention to details, and efficiency under pressure.
I see it as a wonderful way to combine work and creativity.


Photography

I have a passion for photography, capturing moments
and telling stories through images. This interest of mine
allows me to explore my creative side and see the world
from different perspectives. I enjoy taking pictures of view
such as the skies, sunset, and flowers. This offers a
wonderful balance to my academic pursuits.


Music

Music is my passion. It offers a creative balance to my
analytical pursuits. I am interested in roles that blend
my love for music with my organizational skills, such as
music event management or even working as music librarian.


I am excited about the possibilities of combining my diverse interests.
Whether it is starting a cafe that doubles as a live music venues.
organizing muusic events, or freelancing in library cataloging and
consulting for cafe startups, I am driven by creativity and versatility.
